Fifteen Puzzle

The goal of the 15 puzzle, or n-Puzzle depending on the number of tiles, is to arrange the tiles into numerical order by repeatedly sliding a square that neighbors the missing square into its empty space. You can move any tile that is movable by clicking on that tile, and if that tile is movable the tile will move into the blank slot. Press Shuffle to begin game, and your game time and number of moves will be recorded below.
